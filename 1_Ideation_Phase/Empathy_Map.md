## Key Insights from Empathy Mapping

### Insight 1: Personalization is Everything
Users don't want generic plans. They need something that considers their **specific body, goals, and schedule**.

### Insight 2: Adaptability Reduces Drop-Off
The #1 reason people quit fitness plans is **rigidity**. A system that adapts to feedback keeps users engaged.

### Insight 3: Nutrition is Inseparable from Exercise
Users know they need to "eat healthy" but don't know **what specifically** to eat for their goal.

### Insight 4: Simplicity Wins
Complex apps with 50 features are intimidating. Users want a **clean, focused experience**.

### Insight 5: Motivation Needs to Be Built-In
Daily motivational notes and visual design play a crucial role in **sustained engagement**.

---

## How FitBuddy Addresses These Insights

| Insight | FitBuddy Solution |
|---|---|
| Needs personalization | Profile-based AI prompt generates unique plan |
| Plan is too rigid | Feedback tab to update plan with natural language |
| Confused about nutrition | Dedicated Nutrition Tips tab with Gemini AI |
| App is too complex | Single-page app with 3 clear tabs |
| Needs motivation | Motivational notes in each day card of the plan |
